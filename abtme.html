A breif history

I was born in Belgium (Veurne) on the 29th July 1991, which makes me 26 years old now.
At the age of 6 my parents moved to Kenya as they were in the flower industry, so along I came, with my sister.
After graduating I went straight into the professional world, where I joined my parents company and also worked for a water driling company in Ethiopia.

Since 2015 I own Decomagna ltd, an import company of interior products from Belgium.

We also manufacture furniture & do interior fitouts.

WHY AM I A STUDENT AT MORINGA?
